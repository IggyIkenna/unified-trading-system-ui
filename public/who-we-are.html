Who We Are

Odum Research is a regulated quantitative trading firm.

We manage selected systematic strategies — whether developed by Odum, shaped with partners, or operated through our infrastructure for institutional clients.

What we do

Three engagement routes. One operating system.

Public engagements run through three routes — capital allocation to Odum-managed strategies, the underlying trading infrastructure, or a regulated operating model where the engagement requires it. Everything runs on a single platform: the same system we use to trade our own capital.

Odum-Managed Strategies

Allocate capital to selected systematic strategies managed by Odum across digital assets, traditional markets, sports, and prediction markets. FCA-regulated reporting and oversight, available through SMA or fund-route structures where appropriate.

DART Trading Infrastructure

The trading infrastructure behind Odum’s own systematic strategies — research, execution, reconciliation, and reporting in one coherent system. Available to selected clients with client-provided signals, Odum-provided signals, or hybrid workflows depending on the engagement.

Regulated Operating Models

Where the engagement requires governance, supervisory reporting, FCA coverage, SMA routes, or affiliate fund pathways, the operating model is structured around the right shape. Assessed case by case; not every engagement requires or qualifies for the same route.

Why institutions work with us

The combination matters more than any single capability.

Most firms offer one piece: strategies, or parts of a trading platform, or a regulatory wrapper. Even among trading-platform providers, few combine backtest, execution, reconciliation, and analytics in a single coherent system. IP protection is often absent, and at some providers the platform is built by engineers who’ve never traded through it themselves.

Odum provides all three engagement routes — Odum-Managed Strategies, DART Trading Infrastructure, and Regulated Operating Models — on a single operating system. DART can additionally support Odum-provided signals leased to counterparties who prefer to execute on their own stack. Built by traders who actually ran desks at leading prop-trading firms, investment banks, and regulated financial institutions. Backtest and live run on the same code path, and your IP stays with you by design.

Clients access the same system we use to manage real capital. There is no separate “client version” of the platform. The strategies, data pipelines, execution infrastructure, and risk controls are identical.

Breadth of coverage

Crypto exchanges, DeFi protocols, traditional markets, sports, and prediction markets through one unified stack. Most competitors focus on a single asset class.

Same system, no watered-down version

You access the exact platform, strategies, and infrastructure that Odum uses to trade its own capital. Research, execution, risk, and reporting — all shared.

Real results from real capital

Decades of combined experience at leading quantitative firms and investment banks, with a live track record managing actual capital — not just backtests.

Regulated from day one

FCA authorisation (975797) from inception. Investment management, reporting, compliance, and client oversight built into the operating model, not bolted on afterwards.

Regulatory and client scope

Who we serve and how we operate

Odum Research Ltd is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), firm reference number 975797. Our public engagement routes are Odum-Managed Strategies, DART Trading Infrastructure, and Regulated Operating Models — structured case by case under our existing FCA permissions.

Our client base is limited to Professional Clients and Eligible Counterparties as defined under FCA rules. We do not offer services to retail investors.
