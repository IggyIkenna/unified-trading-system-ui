Three engagement routes & site spaces

The public site offers three engagement routes: Odum-Managed Strategies (Investment Management under FCA-regulated wrapper), DART Trading Infrastructure (one platform with client-provided / Odum-provided / hybrid signal capability), and Regulated Operating Models (FCA-authorised operating coverage assessed case by case).

  • Public — zero-auth marketing (/, /investment-management, /platform, /regulatory, /who-we-are, /contact, /start-your-review).
  • Lighter gate — /briefings pre-commitment narrative; optional invite code via NEXT_PUBLIC_BRIEFING_ACCESS_CODE; separate session key from staging gate and Firebase.
  • Investor relations — entitled decks under /investor-relations/* with granular keys plus optional investor-archive.
  • Signed-in platform (Odum-Managed Strategies) — strategy catalogue and performance surfaces (/services/research/strategy/catalog, /services/im/funds).
  • Signed-in platform (DART) — operational stack (data, research, build, trading, reporting, observation, signals) starting at /dashboard.
Authentication model

Public and lighter gate do not require Firebase. Platform and Investment management share the same signed-in session. Staging may use NEXT_PUBLIC_STAGING_AUTH and demo personas — see the UI repo docs/DEPLOYMENT.md for build-time flags.
