Who we work with

Each client path maps to a specific Odum offering — not a bundle. Hover the bolded term to see how the path fits.

Allocators & family offices

Your edge is capital allocation, not strategy research. You allocate; we run the strategies. Investment Management mandate under FCA cover; reporting partitioned to your slice.

Professional trading firms

Your edge is alpha; building every operational layer eats the edge. DART Full puts research, promotion, and live execution on the same stack Odum uses internally, under a client-exclusivity contract.

Emerging managers with their own strategies

You have the signal; you don't yet have the regulated execution and reporting wrapper. DART Signals-In takes your instructions into our execution, reconciliation, analytics, and reporting — optionally under Regulatory Umbrella cover.

Institutions & platforms

You have a product, clients, and a brand. Embed Odum signals or platform components — full-code, low-code, or no-code, your frontend or ours. One integration, one commercial relationship.
