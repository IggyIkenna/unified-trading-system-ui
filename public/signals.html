Odum Signals — Odum → counterparty

Odum Signals — our signals, your execution: Odum-generated signals, delivered to your execution stack.

Backend-first integration for institutional quant shops running their own execution. Odum generates and broadcasts signals; the counterparty executes on its own infrastructure. A light observability UI ships alongside (signal history, backtest comparison, delivery health); the primary integration is API + webhook.

Direction matters. Odum Signals runs Odum → counterparty: our signals flow OUT to your execution, and Odum does not see your fills, positions, or P&L. The inverse path is DART Signals-In, where your instructions flow INTO our execution, reconciliation, and reporting layer.

How the feed works

Signals are emitted from the same strategy-service runtime Odum uses for its own capital. A counterparty endpoint is provisioned with a shared HMAC secret and a slot-level entitlement list. Each signal is signed, idempotency-tagged, and delivered over webhook with at-least-once retry semantics.

1

Entitlement-scoped

Per-counterparty allowlist of strategy slots. No signal leaves Odum's scope unless the counterparty is explicitly entitled to that slot.

2

Signed + idempotent

HMAC-signed payload with a per-signal UUID. Delivery is at-least-once with idempotency-key dedup on your side.

3

Failure-isolated

Counterparty endpoint failures never impact Odum strategy-service or any other counterparty. Per-venue failure isolation applies.

4

Audited + metered

Every emission + acknowledgement logged. Billing reads from the audit log; counterparties receive a monthly summary.

Example payload

Standard schema depth. Fields vary per negotiated contract; minimal and rich depths available.

What we explicitly do not do

Odum Signals is not raw market-data resale and not execution outsourcing. We keep these boundaries tight.

  • We do not resell raw market data. Signals are enriched output; underlying venue data is not redistributed as a feed.
  • We do not see your positions or fills. You execute on your own infrastructure. No return-reporting required.
  • We do not disclose methodology. Signal generation, feature logic, and model internals stay on Odum's side of the fence.
  • We do not permit onward-resale. Signals are licensed to the named counterparty only; onward distribution is prohibited by contract.
  • We do not guarantee profitability. Signals reflect Odum's strategy-service output; counterparty bears execution and market risk.

Commercial model

Hybrid — monthly licence floor plus per-signal metering, with an optional P&L revenue-share on Tier B engagements. Fixed twelve-month minimum commitment.

Monthly licence floor

Fixed monthly per-counterparty per signal-scope. Covers authenticated endpoint, audit log access, and a bundled signal volume.

Per-signal uplift

Metered pricing on signals-delivered above the bundled volume. Transparent, auditable via the emission log.

Optional P&L share

Tier B engagements can opt into a revenue-share on counterparty's signal-attributable P&L, with audit rights on the attribution flow.

Exact pricing is negotiated per counterparty based on scope (single signal family vs bundled multi-family vs full catalogue) and schema depth. Contact us to discuss.
