About Odum Research

A regulated quantitative trading firm spanning investment management, platform infrastructure, and regulatory services

Odum Research is an FCA-authorised firm that trades systematic strategies across digital assets, traditional markets, sports, and prediction markets — and provides the same platform and regulatory infrastructure to institutional clients.

What we do

Systematic strategy allocation across five asset groups. FCA-regulated reporting, oversight, and a clear mandate process for professional and eligible counterparty clients.

Platform

Regulatory and client scope

Who we serve and how we operate

Odum Research Ltd is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), firm reference number 975797. We operate as an investment manager, provide trading platform infrastructure, and offer regulatory coverage through structured engagement under our existing FCA permissions.

Our client base is limited to Professional Clients and Eligible Counterparties as defined under FCA rules. We do not offer services to retail investors.

The firm is headquartered in London, United Kingdom.
