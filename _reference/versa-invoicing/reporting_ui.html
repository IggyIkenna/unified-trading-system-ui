Fee Structures
Global Fee Rules

Two separate HWMs: Odum HWM (client invoice history) and Trader HWM (trader payouts). Never merge.

Fees only above HWM. Losses never generate fees. HWM never reduces except on net withdrawals.

Trader fee: 10% of gross strategy PnL. Accrued independently of client billing.

Odum fee: Charged only when AUM > Odum HWM. On invoice issuance, Odum HWM advances to current AUM.

Introducer fee: % of fees actually collected (not accrued). Only after client payment.

Underwater: $50/month server cost to trader. No performance fees until HWM recovery.

Trader credits: If trader was paid but client fees refunded → credit deducted at next recovery.
